1. Overview

SOCO-T1 is a three-phase Solar and Grid Controller designed for industrial and telecom applications where load must be managed between a mains grid supply and a solar supply. The controller continuously measures both sources independently, manages relay-driven contactors to switch loads, and exposes all data over an isolated Modbus RTU (RS-485) interface.

Telecom tower panels Solar-grid hybrid systems Automatic source changeover Industrial power management
2. Key Features
▶ Simultaneous, independent measurement of Grid and Solar sources — both three-phase, four-wire
▶ Grid and Solar (individual) measurement: voltage, current, active/reactive/apparent power, power factor, energy (4-quadrant), frequency, neutral current
▶ 6 relay outputs (SPDT, potential-free) for driving contactors and fan control
▶ 8 potential-free digital inputs with isolated internal supply for contactor feedback monitoring
▶ Configurable over/under voltage thresholds and fail/return time delays independently for Grid and Solar sources
▶ Ambient temperature measurement with fan control relay
▶ Fan current monitoring on two channels for open/short-circuit fault protection
▶ Isolated RS-485 Modbus RTU communication
▶ Dual auxiliary supply: 40–60 V DC and 100–300 V AC
▶ 6-digit alphanumeric LED display
